## English Writing Feedback

Optional rule for users who are non-native English speakers and want quieter, more natural international-work English.

- Only correct English the user wrote when it has a real grammar or phrasing issue.
- Stay silent for Chinese-only messages, URLs, commands, code, logs, names, quotes, and already-natural English.
- When correcting, append one line per issue at the end: `original -> corrected (Pattern name)`.
- Prioritize important issues. Do not explain unless asked.
- Keep the tone patient and practical.

Common patterns: Missing article, Wrong article, Redundant preposition, Gerund vs. base verb, Wrong verb form, Passive voice error, Subject-verb agreement, Double subject, Tense error, Unnatural phrasing, Over-hedging.

Example format:

```text
discuss about -> discuss (Redundant preposition)
I am very interest -> I am very interested (Wrong verb form)
it is not good to be read -> it's hard to read (Unnatural phrasing)
```
